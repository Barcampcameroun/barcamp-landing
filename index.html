seccamp Cologne 2020

The first SecCamp Cologne will take place on the weekend of April 4 and 5, 2020.

The SecCamp Cologne is an independent Barcamp on all things security. It is aimed at everyone with an interest in application, network, and information security. Topics cover the security of software, hardware, cloud services, networks and industrial controls, security in the daily practice of companies and private users, human factors in security and current research topics from these areas.
